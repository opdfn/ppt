Summary

NCFSE ২০২৩ মানে আমি কি বুজোঁ?

The National Curriculum Framework for School Education (NCFSE) 2023 is developed by NCERT under the guidance of the Ministry of Education. It is based on the vision of the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020. It acts like a roadmap to implement NEP 2020 in schools. 👉 Simple idea: NEP 2020 = What to do (vision) NCFSE 2023 = How to do it (implementation)

  • 🎯
    Alignment: Direct output of the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020.
  • 📈
    Coverage: Ages 3 to 18 years, encompassing 15 years of schooling.
  • 🧩
    Focus: Holistic, flexible, and multidisciplinary education.
  • 🌍
    Inclusion: Emphasis on learning in home languages.

Vision: পঞ্চকোষ বিকাশত আমাক কেনেদৰে সহায় কৰিব ?

The New 5+3+3+4 Structure মানে কি বুজোঁ?

  • Foundational (5 Yrs): Ages 3-8 , Classes: Pre-school (3 years --> Anganwadi) + Class 1–2 [খেলাধুলাভিত্তিক শিক্ষাৰ ওপৰত গুৰুত্ব। বেছি কৈ দিছে মানে ]
  • Preparatory (3 Yrs): Ages 8-11 [Focus on basic -- reading, writing, mathematics skill]
  • Middle Stage (3 Yrs): Ages 11-14 [Focus on - experiential learning]
  • Secondary Stage (4 Yrs): Ages 14-18 [Focus on critical thinking & career readiness]

কিয় এইটো গুৰুত্বপূৰ্ণ? Covers early childhood education (age 3+) Focus on holistic development (Panchakosha Vikas) Reduces rote learning, increases understanding Aligns with child psychology

Old Structure vs নতুন structure পাৰ্থক্য

10 + 2 System

Problem: No focus on early childhood (age 3–6) More rote learning (ratta maro system) Less focus on development stages of child

New Structure (After NEP 2020 → NCFSE 2023)

✔ Early childhood education is now part of formal education ✔ Focus on Panchakosha Vikas (overall development) ✔ Learning based on child psychology, not just syllabus

Old system (10+2) ignored early childhood, while new 5+3+3+4 structure includes ages 3–18 and is based on developmental stages of children.

আধুনিক শিক্ষা নীতি ক’ত ক’ত Focus কৰিছে?

Moving beyond Rote Learning:

  • Experiential: Hands-on learning & field trips.
  • Integrated: Using Arts & Sports to teach core subjects.
  • Digital: Smart classrooms and AI-enabled tools.
  • Modular: Biannual Board Exams to reduce stress.
